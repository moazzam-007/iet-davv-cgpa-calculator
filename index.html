How to Use this Calculator

📖 Theory Subjects

The calculator automatically loads the standard syllabus for E&TC. If you are from another branch, simply click on the subject names and credits to change them. Your changes are saved automatically!

🔬 Practicals

Practicals usually carry 1 or 2 credits and count towards your SGPA. Just click the "+ Add Extra Subject / Viva" button to add a practical row, set the credits to 1 or 2, and select your grade.

🗣 Comprehensive Viva

According to the IET DAVV ordinance, Comprehensive Viva carries 4 virtual credits. Click the "+ Add" button to easily add this to your calculation.

🔄 SGPA to CGPA Sync

Once you calculate your term SGPA, a "Save to CGPA Table" button will appear. Clicking it will safely copy that SGPA and total credits into the CGPA Calculator tab.
